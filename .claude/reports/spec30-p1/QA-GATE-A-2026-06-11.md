## Defect found + fixed during the gate

**WC 10's "store coming soon" mode was ON** (`woocommerce_coming_soon=yes`, WC 10 default on fresh installs) — all store pages served the Coming Soon template to guests, masking the theme templates entirely. Set to `no` on the canary. **Operator note for client launches: this option is part of go-live (FR-30-13 should mention it).**
