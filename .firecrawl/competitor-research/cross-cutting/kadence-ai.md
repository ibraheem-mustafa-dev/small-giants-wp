## Great questions

What is Kadence AI?

Kadence AI is a website design and content generation tool for WordPress that lives in Kadence Blocks and Kadence Starter Templates. See [documentation](https://www.kadencewp.com/help-center/docs/kadence-ai/what-is-kadence-ai/) for more information.

How does Kadence AI work?

Kadence AI simplifies the content creation process. Once you supply basic information about your business, our technology takes over and generates relevant copy that tells your story and speaks to your goals. You don’t need any technical expertise to use our AI features – simply focus on your business, and we’ll handle the rest. Powered by OpenAI, Kadence AI, creates a professional-grade website faster and easier than ever before.

Is Kadence AI Free?

Kadence AI is free to try with a limited number of AI credits, specifically 250. However many patterns and AI starter layouts are only available for pro users. Currently, 400+ Patterns in the design library are pro-only.

What is a Credit?

Credits are how we manage the amount of times you can prompt the AI to generate/edit content on your site. They provide a way to prevent abuse and manage sane usage limits.

Free users get 250 lifetime credits, for pro users the credit amounts depend on your purchase.

Credits are “spent” when you ask the AI to do something. For example, if you are asking the AI to improve upon a headline you’ve written that costs one credit. Suppose you want the AI to generate content for an entire context in the design library that will cost more usually around four credits. Each context differs depending on how many prompts are needed to fill out a context. Filling out the entire context library costs around 100 credits.

Users will have opportunities to buy and add credits to their accounts in the future; prices for this are not yet set but will probably be $20 for 4000-lifetime credits.

What happens if I run out of credits

Credits are how we manage the amount of times you can prompt the AI to generate/edit content on your site. They provide a way to prevent abuse and manage sane usage limits.

Free users get 250 lifetime credits, for pro users the credit amounts depend on your purchase.

If you run out of credits as a free user you will need to purchase one of our premium products to continue using Kadence AI.

If you run out of credits as a pro user, you will have opportunities to buy addon credits in your account in the future; prices for this are not yet set but will probably be $20 for 4000-lifetime credits.

Are the images provided by Kadence in the Design Library royalty-free?

Yes! All images provided are fully royalty-free, so you can use them however you want!

Can I edit the content generated by Kadence AI?

Absolutely! It is meant to be tweaked and customized to fit your brand perfectly.

Can I use my own images and still use the AI feature?

Yes. Feel free to swap out any AI generated image with your own.
