# Google Reviews Features

Explore all the features of Google business reviews and how they can help you create the most effective widget.

### Integrate Google reviews in 1-2-3

In case you have a Google profile, where users leave their comments and reviews, it’s a must-do to integrate this feedback into your website. With Elfsight Google Reviews, all you need to do is to get authorized on Google as a page admin. The widget will automatically find the reviews and publish them on your site. It’s also auto-updating, so immediately after a new review is published on Google, it will show on your site, too.

### Manage your content right with our filters

Elfsight Google Reviews will help your reveal only positive or the most meaningful client reviews about your business or items you sell. For this, there are three filters in the widget. You can choose to show all reviews or positive only; Exclude By filter removes reviews including the keywords that you set; and with the help of quantity filter, you can choose the number of reviews to show.

### Ratings and author details for higher confidence

Reviews are one of the best ways to prove to clients that your company is the one to trust, that’s why they must look trustworthy, too. With Elfsight widget, website visitors will see not just the text of the recommendation, but maximum convincing info: author’s name, picture, the date of posting, a Google and recommendation icons, and a star rating. And all of them are switchable. Having well-known and familiar Google design features, your reviews will be flawlessly trustworthy!

reviews

### Widget header will help show important details

With our widget, you can use social factor to shape positive image of your business among website visitors. The header of Elfsight Google Reviews widget reveals overall star and number ratings that people gave you, the total amount of clients who recommended you, and photo of the latest of them. Clicking on those pictures and the name of your place, visitors will get redirected to your Google Page, where they can leave their own review.

### Configure the header yourself – it’s flexible

The header of Elfsight Google Reviews is absolutely flexible and you are free to design it your own way. You can show or hide ratings and pictures of the latest reviewers and set the color of the number rating. The title of the header is editable, so you can think of your own name and choose the font and weight. If you don’t need the header, you can easily switch it off.

### Review request button for fresh reviews

Our widget helps not just to demonstrate reviews, but it also to get fresh ones. Call-to-action button offers visitors to write their own recommendation and has Yes and No variants. If a visitor is ready to add a review, then Yes button will redirect them to your Google page. No button will either simply hide the request, or show a custom message. In the message, you can enter any text and links, for example your email, and ask a visitor to send their insights to you. Have more client insights using just a single review widget.

feedback

### Multiple review templates designed for various cases

With Elfsight tool, your Google reviews can look sleek, yet keep the details of well-recognized Google design. We offer three templates that will present your reviews each in its own way. While Classic template will save original Google look, Bubble will set author info under the review text, and Spotlight will place all the elements center and put the accent on the text. It’s easy to display reviews from a fresh point of view!

### 6 options of widget layout

There are six versions of layout at your service, so you will be able to determine the best area of your reviews on the pages of your website. They may be ordered one by one by means of List option; pick Grid to build a grid of testimonials; Slider will demonstrate reviews carousel; Masonry will put reviews in best possible position according to available vertical space; Floating Badge will follow your website visitor as he scrolls the page, and the Embeddable badge is there to seamlessly blend the review cards into the site design. Each layout offers extra settings: by means of Slider, you can choose arrows or drags for navigation, choose sliding speed and pagination option, and with every layout, you can choose the number of reviews to show per page.

### Set your custom length and width

To ideally fit your reviews into the page, with any space requirements, we have created this widget to be totally adaptive. Choose height and width in pixels or percentage. Try all combinations and find the most suitable. In addition, with any parameters you choose, your Google Reviews will look great on tablets and mobile phones because the widget has inbuilt mobile optimization.

Design

### Fonts and weights parameters

The text is one of the most important parts of the widget, and you can choose the size of the font yourself. The option is supported for the widget title text and for the text of the reviews, so you can set them independently. For the title text, you can also choose regular or bold variant of the font.

### Elements of the interface that you can paint

With the widget by Elfsight, your Google reviews just can’t look boring. There are five elements that you can color individually: review text, review background, links, review request button, and a star rating. Try out different combinations or keep the familiar Google colors – it’s up to you!
