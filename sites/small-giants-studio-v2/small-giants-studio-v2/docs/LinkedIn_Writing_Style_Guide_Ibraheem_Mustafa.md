## QUALITY CHECK BEFORE POSTING

Ask yourself:

1. **Does it celebrate others or just me?**
2. **Is there a values-based takeaway?**
3. **Did I tag specific people who deserve credit?**
4. **Is there a moment of vulnerability or self-awareness?**
5. **Would I actually say this in a conversation?**
6. **Are my hashtags specific, not generic?**
7. **Is it under 300 words unless it needs to be longer?**
8. **Is the opening emotional or relational, not sales-y?**
