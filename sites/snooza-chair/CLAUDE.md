## Product: The Snooza Chair

Medical/rehabilitation seating for children and adults with epilepsy, autism, cerebral palsy. Foam construction with medical-grade vinyl, breathable washable cover.

**Configurations:**
- 6 colours: Mandarin Orange, Royal Blue, Apple (green), Grey, Hot Pink, Black
- 4 sizes: Size 1 (12-24 months) through Size 4 (adult)
- Standard kit: headrest, breathable wrap, padded hip belt, single wedge

**Accessories (sold separately):**
1. Rocker Base
2. Snooza Lite (lightweight version)
3. Mobile Base
4. Pommel (Medial Thigh Support)
5. Leg Rest
6. Profile Headrest
7. Padded Tray
8. Side Infill Pads (pair)
9. Base Wedge
10. Back Rest Adjustment

**Pricing:** From £1,164.71 ex VAT. Accessories priced separately.
