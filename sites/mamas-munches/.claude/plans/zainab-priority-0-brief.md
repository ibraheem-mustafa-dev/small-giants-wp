# Mama's Munches — Priority 0: Demand Validation Taste-Test
**For:** Zainab
**Time to complete:** 15 minutes of your time
**Why it matters:** This single test is the cheapest insurance against building something nobody buys.

---

## What you need to do

**DM 5 mum-friends in your Birmingham network today.**

Use the WhatsApp script below. You want a mix — not just your closest friends, and not only Pakistani mums. Include whoever honestly knows other new mums or breastfeeding mums.

---

## WhatsApp script (copy and send)

> "Hey [name] 👋 Hope you and baby are well!
>
> I'm working on something I'd love your honest opinion on. I've been baking lactation cookies — oats, flaxseed, brewer's yeast, the real galactagogue ingredients — and I want to know if actual mums would actually buy them or not.
>
> I'll send you a free box — no catch, just tell me honestly what you think after you try them. Would that be okay?
>
> Also two quick questions before I send:
> 1. Would you ever buy lactation cookies for yourself, or would you not bother?
> 2. Would £10 for 8 big cookies feel reasonable, or too expensive?
>
> No right answers — I genuinely just want to know. Even if the answer is 'I'd just make my own oat biscuits' that's useful 😊"

---

## After they reply

Once they've tried the cookies, send:

> "So honest question — what did you actually think? About the taste, the texture, and whether you'd buy them or recommend them to a breastfeeding friend?"

---

## What to log (takes 2 min per reply)

Fill this in for each person:

| Q | Options |

| **Is £10 for 8 cookies reasonable?** | Yes / Too expensive / Depends on quality |
| **First reaction after tasting** | (their words — quote directly) |
| **Would you recommend to a friend?** | Yes / Maybe / No |
| **Any comments on flavour, texture, or ingredients** | (their words) |

---

## What you're testing

1. **Do breastfeeding mums actually want lactation cookies — or do they just make their own oat biscuits / rely on family?**
2. **Is £10 for 8 Zookies a price people accept, or do they flinch?**
3. **Does the taste pass? (If it doesn't, nothing else matters.)**

---

## What happens with the results

If 3+ out of 5 say yes or maybe → we proceed with the full product launch plan.

If 3+ out of 5 say no or flag price → we adjust pricing or reconsider the product before building anything.

This test costs a box of cookies and 15 minutes. The alternative is building a full website and spending money on packaging and certifications before knowing if people want to buy it.
