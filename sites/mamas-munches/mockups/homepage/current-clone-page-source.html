What’s in them?

Proper ingredients, properly used

Every Zookie contains the same four galactagogues that appear in postpartum food traditions across cultures.

Oats

Rich in iron. Used in postpartum foods across cultures for centuries.

Brewer’s Yeast

B-vitamins and chromium. A traditional galactagogue used worldwide.

Flaxseed

Omega-3 fatty acids. Good for mum and baby.

Fenugreek

Traditional postpartum herb. If your baby shows GI sensitivity, speak to your midwife first.
